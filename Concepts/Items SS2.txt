
Item Concept SS2 

FindMyItem Function (ItemID)

Items will now only have 2 Slots - misc equips, with 4 itemslot spaces.

p1.is.4=ITEM_ID
Item Slot

p1.es.2=ITEM_ID
Equip Slot

p1.ei.3=ENCHANT_ID
Enchantment ID

Enchantments will be attached to the slot, though the only way to unequip the item is to destroy it.
